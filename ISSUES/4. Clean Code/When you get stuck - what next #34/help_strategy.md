When do you prefer using AI vs. searching Google?
Developers often use ChatGPT or similar AI tools when they need quick explanations, examples, or help understanding concepts.
How do you decide when to ask a colleague instead?
Developers usually ask a colleague when:

The problem is specific to the project architecture

The codebase is large or complex

The issue involves team conventions or internal systems

They have already tried debugging and researching without success

Colleagues often have context about the system, which makes it easier to identify the problem quickly.

What challenges do developers face when troubleshooting alone?
When developers work alone on a problem, they may face several challenges:

Limited perspective
A developer might overlook the problem because they are too focused on one approach.

Time consumption
Debugging alone can take longer without feedback or collaboration.

Misinterpreting errors
Error messages can sometimes be confusing or misleading.

Lack of context
Without help, it can be harder to understand how different parts of a system interact.
